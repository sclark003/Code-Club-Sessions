The micro:bit has a magnetometer - a sensor that detects magnetic fields, including Earth's magnetic field! This lets it work like a compass, telling you which direction you're facing. Directions are measured in degrees: 0Â° = North, 90Â° = East, 180Â° = South, 270Â° = West.
